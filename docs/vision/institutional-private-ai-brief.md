# Meta Agents Harness  
## ICP Brief  
### Institutional Private AI / “Institutional Jarvis”

**Document type:** ICP + offer brief  
**Status:** Product strategy brief

---

## 1. ICP Summary

A highly promising ICP for Meta Agents Harness is **private institutions that want to build an institutional “Jarvis”**:
- local or private-cloud inference
- private RAG over internal knowledge
- agentic workflows tied to institution-specific processes
- secure orchestration, guardrails, scopes, and auditability

This is not just “an internal chatbot.”  
It is a **private institutional agent system** with governance.

---

## 2. Core Pain

Institutions can often assemble:
- a RAG
- an internal chatbot
- or a few workflow automations

But they struggle to build:
- coordinated specialist agent teams
- safe delegation by domain
- strong policy boundaries
- explainability and provenance
- integration between private inference and real institutional processes

---

## 3. Product Framing

Meta Agents Harness can be positioned here as:

> **The secure orchestration layer behind your institution’s private Jarvis.**

Alternative framing:

- Institutional Agent Operating Layer
- Private Agentic Control Layer for Institutional AI
- Secure orchestration for private, domain-aware, agentic systems

---

## 4. Example Agent Team

A private institutional “Jarvis” is best modeled not as one monolithic assistant, but as an orchestrated team:

- **Institutional Orchestrator** — receives requests and routes work
- **Policy / Compliance Agent** — verifies access, scope, and sensitive boundaries
- **Knowledge Agent** — queries internal RAG sources
- **Process Agent** — executes approved workflows
- **Systems Agent** — interfaces with internal systems under policy
- **Validation Agent** — reviews response/action quality
- **Confidential Mode** — used for high-sensitivity tasks

---
